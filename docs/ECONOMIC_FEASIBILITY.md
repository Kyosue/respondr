## Discussion

### Cost Analysis

The total implementation cost of the system amounts to **Php 67,344.00**, which includes:

- **FireBase Annual Subscription (Php 25,344.00)**: Cloud infrastructure and backend services for the application
- **User Training (Php 3,000.00)**: Conduct of training sessions for end users
- **User Evaluation (Php 1,000.00)**: Assessment and feedback collection from users
- **User Manual Development (Php 2,000.00)**: Creation and distribution of documentation
- **Annual Maintenance and Updates (Php 36,000.00)**: Ongoing system maintenance, bug fixes, and feature updates

### Benefits Analysis

The system provides significant time savings by reducing manual processes from 10 hours to 2 hours per task. This translates to:

- **Monthly Savings**: Php 11,165.45
- **Annual Savings**: Php 133,985.4

### Financial Metrics

- **Return on Investment (ROI)**: 98.96%
  - This indicates that for every peso invested, the organization receives nearly double the return in benefits.

- **Payback Period**: 6.03 months
  - The system will recover its initial investment cost within approximately 6 months of operation.

### Conclusion

The economic feasibility analysis demonstrates that the system is financially viable, with a positive ROI of 98.96% and a payback period of just over 6 months. The annual benefits (Php 133,985.4) significantly outweigh the total cost (Php 67,344.00), making this a sound investment for the organization.
